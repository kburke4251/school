Roy Baboon

I have been Principle at Baboon High School since it was founded. It only seems like yesterday that I started, but things have changed a lot during that time as we have grown from one student to six students. Every day is different in my role as principle and it's never boring! I love leading the school, challenging and supporting staff and children to be the best they can be. We have a great team of teachers and support staff at Baboon High and I think the six children we teach are very lucky to be in such a prestigious school. As many of you will know, I am passionate about sustainability and I am very proud that Baboon High is seen as a role model by other high schools.
