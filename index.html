Our Mission

Baboon High seeks to create an environment in which any and all students feel welcome and are accepted. Our teachers and staff members are highly trained and commit themselves towards their jobs. Baboon High’s goal is to help children escape the bonds of poverty and hopelessness by providing education, life skills, and an environment that cares.
